ABOUT US

The heaven Company


The Establishment

Since 2008 - The Heaven has established itself as one of the top Food & Beverage entities in the Red Sea - Egypt, which is truly an outstanding achievement which we are justifiably very proud of. The Heaven measures it’s continuous success by the ability to recognize further business opportunities. As well as having extraordinary determination, flair and passion for what we do. All of these attributes together truly define who we are. As business continues to soar to greater heights, our brand reflects our beliefs that the sky is not the limit and that we can always strive for more. We thrive for new and exciting challenges and are fiercely protective of our market leadership. As world-class, we mix the very best international practices with our local market knowledge and expertise. The Heaven have an exceptional team of highly experienced professionals. Each individuals commitment and dedication towards us being the best in the business keeps us motivated and driven to always look onwards. The Heaven’s family are our greatest asset and hope for our promising future. The Heaven is an Egyptian company that specializes in Restaurants, Lounges, Bars and Pubs. Our portfolio is vast accompanying and satisfying our customers with International fine dining Cuisines - (Italian, Swiss & Indian). In addition to providing our customers with many flavorful Beverages that are rival in the market. We always monitor our product diversity by creating products people love and desire. Our main focus is always on providing top class quality, making sure our guests always have an amazing dining experience with us. Creating twists in our food presentation and providing diverse variety really helps us to do that and are our key assets for us to entertain our customers. We love our guests to experience each and every aspect of their visit to us. Starting from the ambience, atmosphere, presentation to the unforgettable indulgent taste of our high standard food what we provide. We feel giving this experience really makes us stand out and allows us to build great relationships with our customers making them our loyal repeater guests.
